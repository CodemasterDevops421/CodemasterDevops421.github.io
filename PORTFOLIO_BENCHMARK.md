## What you're missing (priority-ordered)

### P0 — Must add for serious consulting conversion
1. **3 deep case studies (problem → approach → architecture → measurable result).**
   - Include baseline, intervention, tooling, and outcomes (% lead-time reduction, MTTR, deployment frequency).
   - Each case study should include a simple architecture diagram and constraints section.
2. **Productized offers with clear entry points.**
   - Example: `DevOps Maturity Audit (2 weeks)`, `Pipeline Hardening Sprint (4 weeks)`, `Kubernetes Reliability Program (8 weeks)`.
   - Include deliverables, timeline, and target persona.
3. **Proof hardening for hero claims.**
   - Replace broad numbers with attributable metrics and confidence qualifiers (`across X engagements`, `12-month average`).
4. **Conversion flow upgrade.**
   - Add 2 CTA tracks: `Book discovery call` and `Request technical assessment`.
   - Keep contact form, but add qualification fields (team size, cloud provider, current blockers, budget band).

### P1 — High leverage trust improvements
5. **Named testimonials + client identity where allowed.**
   - Even partial (logo + anonymized quote + role + domain) outperforms generic at this level.
6. **Credibility strip near hero.**
   - Certifications, enterprise clients/sectors, speaking/podcast appearances, OSS contribution count.
7. **Downloadable one-page capability statement (PDF).**
   - Decision-makers often share this internally before first call.

### P2 — Growth and differentiation
8. **Niche landing pages by intent.**
   - `Jenkins modernization`, `Apigee CI/CD`, `EKS platform reliability`, `Terraform governance`.
9. **Technical authority artifacts.**
   - Public runbooks, Terraform modules, Helm templates, SLO playbooks, incident postmortem examples.
10. **Measurement and funnel analytics.**
   - Track CTA CTR, form completion rate, qualified lead rate, and source attribution.

## Quality bar for each new case study
- Context: company type, team size, stack, constraints.
- Problem: pre-state operational pain + baseline metrics.
- Implementation: architecture choices, trade-offs, security/compliance controls.
- Outcome: quantified business + engineering impact.
- Reproducibility: checklist/playbook snippets.

## Target state (90 days)
- 3–5 high-quality case studies
- 2–3 productized offers
- 4 niche landing pages
- 1 lead magnet + newsletter capture
- Fully instrumented conversion funnel

Expected effect: higher quality inbound, better close rate, and stronger pricing power due to clearer proof and specialization.
